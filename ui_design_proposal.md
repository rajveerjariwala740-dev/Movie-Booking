# Proposed Movie Details UI Redesigns

Based on your request, I have generated three distinct, ultra-modern UI design concepts for the Movie Details page. Please review the options below and let me know which direction you would like me to build!

---

## Option 1: Ultra-Cinematic Full-Bleed
**Vibe:** Immersive, massive, and theatrical.
- The entire top `70vh` of the screen is a massive, edge-to-edge background of the movie's poster, aggressively darkened so text remains legible.
- The movie details float directly over this backdrop.
- As the user scrolls down, the theaters are presented as wide, expansive horizontal rows rather than vertical cards.

## Option 2: Asymmetrical Split-Screen
**Vibe:** High-fashion, structured, and extremely clean.
- The screen is split vertically. The left half is dedicated entirely to a massive, fixed, high-resolution movie poster.
- The right half is a completely separate, dark-mode scrollable pane containing all the movie data, theater selections, and showtimes.
- Uses sleek glassmorphic cards with vibrant glowing neon accents. Perfect for desktop viewing.

---

## Option 3: Deep Neumorphic Grid
**Vibe:** Sophisticated, dimensional, and futuristic.
- A highly structured, grid-based aesthetic floating on a deep dark void background.
- Elements use "soft" lighting and shadows (neumorphism mixed with glassmorphism) to create a sense of deep Z-axis layering.
- The movie poster is elegantly framed within its own prominent card, surrounded by floating data widgets.
